About Me

I’m Jah-Mali Brathwaite, a passionate software developer and computer science student from Atlanta, Georgia, specializing in C++, C#, and Unity. My love for video games and technology inspired me to pursue a degree in computer science, and I currently work on a video game with my company, Gradient Gaming Studios, which I co-founded. This project has also ignited my interest in digital art, leading me to create custom Twitch emotes for friends who stream. Driven by my curiosity about how technology works, I strive to deepen my knowledge and enhance my skills as a software and game developer. In the future, I hope to see my name in the credits of a popular game and work for leading companies in the tech and gaming industries. In my free time, I enjoy playing games with friends, watching TV (including anime), skating, reading, listening to music, starting my vinyl collection, experimenting with digital art, and participating in community service, such as a recent cleanup with multiple organizations at Morehouse College.

One quote that keeps me motivated during tough times is by Ella Fitzgerald: "Just don’t give up trying to do what you really want to do. Where there is love and inspiration, I don’t think you can go wrong."

Thank you for taking the time to learn more about me.
